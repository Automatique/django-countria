=====================
Django Countria Light
=====================

A full collection of countries, cities, currency, continents and states for Django.

This is a fork of django-countria which removes translations and related dependencies. Information like calling code,
top level domain, population and capital city is also removed.

- Approximately 270 Countries
- Approximately 190,000 Cities
- Approximately 160 Currencies
- Approximately 4200 States

Source:
https://github.com/geomin/django-countria
http://geolite.maxmind.com/download/worldcities/
http://www.andrewpatton.com/countrylist.html
